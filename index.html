About Us

PrimePoint Solar is a renowned Nigerian renewable energy company situated in Lagos. Lack of adequate power supply has always been a major issue in the country, which has proven to stunt economic development and hinder the lives of the masses. The over reliance on fuel as the sole source of powering the nation has proven to have its pitfalls and we must therefore look towards other alternatives. We at PrimePoint Solar are dedicated to tackle this major issue in an environmental and cost-effective manner. We specialize in the installation of solar and inverter systems for homes, hospitals, offices, business and so on. We also sell solar system components like solar panels, batteries, inverters, charge controllers at the best prices you can find anywhere.
